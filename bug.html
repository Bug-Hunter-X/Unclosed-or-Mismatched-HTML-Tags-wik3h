This is a common error, but the root cause can be subtle and tricky to debug. It happens when you use a tag that is not properly closed or nested.

<div>
  <p>This is some text<p>
</div>

In this example, the opening <p> tag has a closing tag </p> but the closing tag is also incorrectly opened <p>, instead of </p>. This can lead to unexpected layout issues or the browser rendering the page incorrectly, and it may be difficult to spot in larger codebases.
